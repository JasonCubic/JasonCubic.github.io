Electric Vehicles

Electric vehicle charging stations are available for employees to recharge their EVs. There are twelve EV charging stations in the parking garage and six stations in the front of Corp HQ. Please use good practices during your EV charging:

  • Plug in if you are parked in an EV spot.
  • Be kind, rewind. Replace the cord neatly on the station after use.
  • Move your car after it's done charging to free up the space for others.
