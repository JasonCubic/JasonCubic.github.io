New Corporate Headquarters Opening Countdown

The new Daimler Trucks North America (DTNA) Corporate Headquarters building, or Corp HQ, formerly known as project NOVA, is on track to for its grand opening on April 19, 2016, with first employees occupying the building on April 25, 2016.

This state-of-the-art building includes a fitness room, coffee shop, cafeteria, modern workspaces, and technologically-advanced conference rooms. The Willamette riverfront and the newly renovated Greenway trail are easily accessible from the building, and there's indoor and outdoor seating and Wi-Fi available.

Corp HQ embodies DTNA's commitment to sustainability by using green technology to create a pleasant, healthy, and energy-efficient work environment.

A New Way to Work

The common and collaborative areas, conference rooms, and workstations at Corp HQ are designed to promote teamwork and collaboration, and utilize forward-looking technology. The building features many shared spaces on every floor, a wide variety of conference rooms, open and airy workstations, and phone booths for private conversations.
