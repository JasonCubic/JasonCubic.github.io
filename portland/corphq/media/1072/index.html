New Corporate Headquarters countdown to opening

If you've been to Portland lately, you've probably seen it: The impressive 9-story building going up on Swan Island is hard to miss. The new Corporate Headquarters building, formerly known as project NOVA, is on track to open in April 2016.

This state-of-the-art, LEED Platinum-certified building offers something for everyone, including a fitness center, coffee shop, and a cafeteria as well as the modern new work spaces and technologically advanced meeting rooms.The Willamette riverfront and the newly renovated Greenway trail are easily accessible from the building, and there will be indoor and outdoor seating for everyone to use. Best of all, it embodies DTNA's commitment to sustainability, using green technology to create a pleasant, healthy, and energy-efficient work environment.

A new way to work

The meeting spaces and workspaces in Corp HQ are designed to promote teamwork, collaboration, and forward-looking technology. The building features a wide variety of shared spaces, including a 300-person capacity conference center, meeting rooms with the latest communication tools, and team spaces with comfortable tables and sofas. The workspaces are open and airy, with well-designed and efficient desks. And because not everything needs to be shared, there are even phone booths for private conversations.
