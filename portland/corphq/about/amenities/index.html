Showers and Lockers

Day use locker rooms with showers are on hand for all DTNA employees. Lockers are not assigned and may be used weekdays. Towels and body wash are provided, and PBM issues locks. See the Building and Facilities User Guidelines as well as the Commuter Communications Board for more information.


Outdoor Activities

The patio is open to everyone and may be reserved for special events. See the PBM Events Site for hours, guidelines, and reservations. Lawn games such as bocce ball, croquet, giant Jenga, ladder golf, and soccer balls may be checked out at the reception desk. Don't miss the giant chess set available seasonally on the patio!


Bike Room

The secure bike storage room is located on the first floor of Corp HQ. The easy-access entrance is located on the east side of the main entry to the building and requires badge access.
