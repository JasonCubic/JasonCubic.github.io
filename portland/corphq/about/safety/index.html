Safety and Security

Safety and Security

As with all DTNA facilities, Corp HQ has access control systems in place to provide for the protection, safety, and security of company personnel and assets. DTNA uses a photo ID security badge system that also functions as a cardkey to gain access to company premises. DTNA security badges must be worn at all times while on company property.

Building Security

The first floor and most of the mezzanine level are accessible to the public during regular business hours (weekdays from 6 A.M. â€“ 6 P.M.) and do not require badge access. DTNA badges are required for access to the bike room, the fitness center, and to access floors two through nine by elevator, stairs, or to enter the building at any of the entrances other than the main entrance. A badge and PIN are required for after-hours access to the building.

Parking Security

The parking garage is accessable weekdays during regular business hours and requires DTNA badge access from 7 P.M. - 6 A.M., and on weekends and holidays. All parking areas are equipped with blue-lighted emergency phones that call Security directly. Security offers an after-hours escort service to your car. You can request an escort to you car in person at the lobby reception desk, or by calling Security at 5-5555.
