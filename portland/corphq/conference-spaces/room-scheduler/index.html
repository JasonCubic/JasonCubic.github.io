Room Scheduler

Room Scheduler

The conference rooms at Corp HQ use an Evoko room scheduling system that's integrated with Outlook. The room scheduler increases conference room availability by using a check-in feature that releases booked conference rooms when no one shows up.

When you add a conference room to your Outlook meeting invite, the room scheduler is automatically updated with the meeting details. When you arrive at the conference room for your meeting, simply confirm on the room scheduler that you're using the room.

See room availability status

You can see at a glance whether or not the room is available by the red or green backlight.

Green = Available

Red = Unavailable

 

 

Reserve a conference room using Outlook

  1. In Outlook, schedule a meeting, including the date and time.
  2. Add Attendees as either Required or Optional, and add the conference room to Resources.
  3. Send the invite.

Confirm room use

Ten minutes before the meeting start time, a Confirm button appears on the room scheduler. If the Confirm button isn't tapped within 15 minutes (five minutes after the scheduled start time), the room reservation is cancelled.

Extend meeting times

If you're already using a room but need additional meeting time, you can easily extend your room reservation using the room scheduler.

  1. Tap anywhere on the screen to go to the Main Menu.
  2. When the room is booked, tap Extend This Meeting and select a proposed end time.

If the meeting has been extended, the red glow will continue. If the meeting has ended, the red glow changes to green, indicating the room is now available.

Reserve a conference room using the room scheduler

Instantly book a meeting while in front of an available room, as indicated by a green glow.

  1. Tap anywhere on the screen to go to the Main Menu.
  2. Tap Book Instant Meeting.
  3. Tap Book Now to select one of four proposed end times.

The green glow changes to red, and the selected end time displays to indicate the room is now booked.

Note: You do not need to tap Confirm after booking an instant meeting.
