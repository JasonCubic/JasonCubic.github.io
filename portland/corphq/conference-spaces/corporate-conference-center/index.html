Corporate Conference Center

Corporate Conference Center

The Corporate Conference Center (CCC) consists of two large rooms. The largest of the two rooms can be divided into three smaller spaces (conference rooms A, B, and C), while the other CCC room can be divided into two smaller spaces (conference rooms D and E). All CCC rooms can be configured in a variety of ways to fit your needs and are equipped with ClickShare for easy screen sharing.

Find information on Corporate Conference Center reservations.

Additional Conference Rooms

In addition to the larger CCC rooms, two smaller conference rooms are also available (Conf 104 in the CCC and Conf M02 on the mezzanine level) and can be booked through Outlook.
