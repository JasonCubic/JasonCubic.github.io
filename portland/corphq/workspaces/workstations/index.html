Workstations

Workstations

All Corp HQ workspaces include an ergonomic sit-and-stand workstation to promote increased circulation, improved mental alertness, and relieve stress on your back and legs. In addition to being comfortable, adjustable standing workstations are convenient. At the push of a button, you can quickly change your sit-down workstation to a standing one.

Point to the different elements in the picture below to learn more about these ergonomic workstations.


Find out more about proper ergonomic sitting and standing positions, or how to adjust your ergonomic task chair.

Guest Workstations

Although there are no dedicated spaces for visitors and guests, floors two through nine have several non-assigned workstations available for use. If you’re working with a specific group at Corp HQ, that group will let you know where there's extra space for you. Remember to log into your phone so that your phone calls and messages are available from your workstation.

Video Phone

All workstations include a video phone with Extension Mobility, which enables your phone number to follow you regardless of which office or conference room you're working in. See the Cisco 8800 Series Phone Guide for information about the video phone features, Extension Mobility log on, and voice mail commands.
